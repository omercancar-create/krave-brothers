Our Story

TWO
BROTHERS.
ONE
OBSESSION.

It started with a cast iron pan, a block of fresh beef, and a bet. Two brothers — one with a chef's hat, one with a cap — decided that the perfect smash burger didn't exist yet. So they made it.

We source fresh, never-frozen beef every single day. We bake our own brioche buns. We make every sauce from scratch. We don't cut corners — we smash them.

🥩
Fresh Daily
Beef ground and delivered every morning. No freezers. No compromises. Ever.
🧀
Real Cheese
American singles, gruyère, smoked gouda — the real stuff, always, no exceptions.
🍞
House Buns
Brioche baked in-house daily. The bun is not an afterthought. Not here.
🔥
Smash Technique
Maximum crust, maximum flavour. Every single patty, every single time.
